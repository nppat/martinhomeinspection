Make an informed decision

A professional home inspection provides the buyer with an opportunity to become familiar with the functions and condition of the home. Every home ages and wears differently and the buyer is best served by having a comprehensive review regardless of the home's age or appearance.


Inspection of a newly constructed home will insure that the builder has not overlooked any details. It will determine if the house meets industry standards or requires any last minute fixes that should be completed before the sale is final. The home inspector works on your behalf to insure you have unbiased information before making that important purchase.
